### **Get Involved: Stream Your Data with OpenAQI**  
Are you a **tech enthusiast, DIY maker, researcher, or someone passionate about clean air?** You can actively contribute to OpenAQI! Our platform allows anyone with an AQI monitoring setup to **stream their real-time air quality data** effortlessly.  

Getting started is simple:  
✅ Connect your sensor to our **web app**  
✅ Start streaming your **live AQI data**  
✅ Help build a **community-driven, real-time air quality network**  

All you need to do is **send a request to [support@wizkit.org]**, and we’ll get you onboarded. Your contribution will help **fill critical data gaps**, making air quality insights accessible to everyone.
